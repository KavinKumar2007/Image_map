Perambur - A Historic Suburb of Chennai

Perambur is one of the oldest and most well-known localities in North Chennai. It is famous for the Integral Coach Factory (ICF), one of the largest railway coach manufacturers in the world. Perambur also has a rich cultural history, vibrant markets, and several educational institutions. Over the years, it has developed into a bustling residential and commercial hub, while still retaining its historic charm and greenery.
