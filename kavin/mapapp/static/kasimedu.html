Kasimedu - Famous Fishing Harbour of Chennai

Kasimedu, located in North Chennai, is well known for its fishing harbour and bustling fish market. Every morning, thousands of fishermen bring in their fresh catch, making it one of the largest seafood hubs in Tamil Nadu. The harbour is also home to hundreds of fishing boats and trawlers.
